Banshee – Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026

Banshee is a browser extension that detects whether the developer studio of a game (on Steam or Instant Gaming) is based in Europe. It is developed independently, with no commercial purpose.

Data collection

Banshee does not collect, store, transmit or sell any personal data. No analytics, no tracking, no third-party services.

Web page content

Banshee reads the text content of game pages (Steam and Instant Gaming) solely to extract the developer studio name. This information is processed locally in your browser and is never sent to any external server.

User preferences

Your settings (language, popup preferences) are saved locally using chrome.storage.local. They never leave your device.

External resources

Banshee loads country flag images from flagcdn.com, a public CDN, for display purposes only. No user data is included in these requests.

Contact

Questions? Reach out via the Banshee Discord.
